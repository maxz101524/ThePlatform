# Project Brief: The Platform - Editorial Powerlifting Hub

**PRD ID:** `bf1ff90440474163b5aa152b696af920`
**Date:** October 26, 2023
**Author:** Product Management Team
**Status:** Design Phase Completed

---

## 1. Executive Summary

This document outlines the scope for **"The Platform - Editorial Powerlifting Hub,"** a new digital application designed to serve as a centralized, high-quality content and community hub for powerlifting enthusiasts. A comprehensive deep design flow has been successfully completed, resulting in a collaborative PRD, refined design direction, and a full set of visually consistent screens that establish the core user experience. This brief details the project's vision, objectives, target audience, and key features.

## 2. Problem Statement

Powerlifting enthusiasts, athletes, and fans currently lack a single, high-quality, and editorially curated platform to access news, in-depth athlete profiles, competition results, and community engagement. Information is often fragmented across various social media, forums, and sport-specific websites, making it challenging to stay updated, track athletes, and engage with the sport meaningfully.

## 3. Goals & Objectives

The primary goals for "The Platform - Editorial Powerlifting Hub" are to:

*   **Centralize Information:** Provide a single, authoritative source for powerlifting news, athlete data, and event information.
*   **Enhance Engagement:** Foster a vibrant community where users can follow athletes, track events, and discuss the sport.
*   **Deliver Quality Content:** Offer editorially curated articles, interviews, and analyses that elevate the standard of powerlifting media.
*   **Improve Discoverability:** Make it easy for users to discover new athletes, learn about upcoming meets, and delve into historical data.
*   **Create a Seamless UX:** Deliver a visually consistent and intuitive user experience across all platform functionalities.

## 4. Target Audience

*   **Powerlifting Athletes:** Both amateur and professional, looking to track their progress, follow competitors, and stay informed.
*   **Coaches & Trainers:** Seeking insights into athlete performance, competition strategies, and sport developments.
*   **Powerlifting Fans & Enthusiasts:** Interested in news, athlete stories, competition results, and community discussions.
*   **Journalists & Media:** Looking for a reliable source of information and data for powerlifting coverage.
*   **Event Organizers:** Potentially as a platform to promote and report on meets (future consideration).

## 5. Key Features / Components

The completed design package includes the following core functionalities, represented by their respective screens:

*   **The Feed (Home)**
    *   **Screen ID:** `b589d0af7fbd499ea332cf131fed7b6a`
    *   **Description:** A personalized or curated home screen displaying the latest news, articles, updates from followed athletes/meets, and trending topics within the powerlifting community. This will be the primary entry point for users to consume content.
*   **Lifter Dossier**
    *   **Screen ID:** `f6bd8ba0bb244a56bf919dd1be52bd12`
    *   **Description:** Detailed profiles for powerlifting athletes, including their personal bests, competition history, biographical information, training insights, and media (photos/videos). Users can follow athletes for updates.
*   **The Leaderboard**
    *   **Screen ID:** `eee4c9c5611f4c28a774685192a20a14`
    *   **Description:** Dynamic rankings and record-keeping across various weight classes, federations, and age groups. Allows users to explore top lifters, track world records, and compare performances.
*   **Meet Hub**
    *   **Screen ID:** `94af99728cfa446eaac07197fdcffe46`
    *   **Description:** A central repository for upcoming and past powerlifting competitions. Includes event details, registration information (if applicable), real-time results during live events, and post-meet summaries.

## 6. Out of Scope (Initial Phase)

*   Direct event registration/payment processing.
*   Personalized training log features (beyond what's displayed in a Lifter Dossier).
*   Monetization features (e.g., premium subscriptions, advertising).
*   Advanced analytics for coaches/athletes (beyond basic stats).
